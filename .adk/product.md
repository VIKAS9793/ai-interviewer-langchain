# AI Interviewer - Product Context

> **Purpose:** Define interview goals, target roles, and evaluation philosophy

---

## Target Roles

### Software Engineering (SWE)
- Entry Level (0-2 years)
- Mid Level (3-5 years)
- Senior (6-10 years)
- Principal/Staff (10+ years)

### Product Management (PM)
- Associate PM
- Product Manager
- Senior PM
- Principal PM

### Data Science & ML
- Entry Data Scientist
- ML Engineer
- Senior DS/ML
- Research Scientist

### System Design Specialist
- Backend Systems
- Distributed Systems
- Infrastructure

---

## Interview Goals

1. **Assess Technical Competency**
   - Core knowledge in chosen domain
   - Problem-solving ability
   - Code quality (for eng roles)

2. **Evaluate Communication**
   - Clarity of explanation
   - Structured thinking
   - Articulation of choices

3. **Test Problem-Solving**
   - Approach to ambiguous problems
   - Trade-off analysis
   - Creativity and adaptability

---

## Evaluation Philosophy

**Fair & Unbiased**
- Judge skills, not demographics
- Safety agent screens for bias
- Consistent rubrics across candidates

**Growth-Oriented**
- Constructive feedback
- Identify strengths AND gaps  
- Actionable improvement suggestions

**Realistic Assessment**
- Industry-standard expectations
- Role-appropriate difficulty
- Time-constrained (like real interviews)

---

## Success Criteria

A successful interview provides:
- ✅ Clear signal on candidate level
- ✅ Specific, actionable feedback
- ✅ Fair assessment across dimensions
- ✅ Professional, respectful experience
